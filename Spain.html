Hotels

The Hat Madrid (Student)

This hotel is honestly perfect for students. It’s super affordable but still really comfortable, and even though it’s a 2-star place, it has everything you actually need. They offer shared rooms, which means you get a great social vibe and plenty of chances to meet people and make awesome memories. You get 24/7 reception, free super-fast Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, secure lockers, and loads of other useful stuff. The Hat Madrid isn’t close to a beach, you’ll need to hop on a bus or train, but it totally makes up for it with the nightlife, with the bar district only about a 10-minute walk away.

Bahia del Duque (Family)

The 5-star Bahía del Duque Resort is located in the stunning Costa Adeje area of Tenerife. Just a short walk from a beautiful beach with crystal-clear waters and soft sand, this resort is perfect for a luxurious family getaway. The hotel offers great facilities, including shops with essentials, stylish air-conditioned rooms, kid-friendly pools, fun activities, and even a babysitting service for parents who want some quiet time.

Parador Corias (Romantic)

The 4-star Parador de Corias is in Cangas del Narcea and mixes modern vibes with amazing natural scenery. They’ve got free Wi-Fi, big comfy rooms with flat-screen TVs, and awesome views that are perfect for relaxing while still having privacy. Bathrooms are spacious with a bathtub, bidet, and free toiletries. The restaurant is fancy and romantic, great for couples, and there's also a spa with a Turkish bath, sauna, and heated indoor pool.

Parador de Fuente Dé (Adventure)

The 3-star Parador de Fuente Dé is right in the middle of the stunning Camaleño National Park, making it perfect for a peaceful mountain getaway. The hotel has a cozy traditional look with lots of wood. Surrounded by mountains and forests, it’s amazing for adventure lovers with epic hikes, breathtaking valley views, and unforgettable sunsets.

Restaurants

El Tigre (Student)

A great budget-friendly spot, this tapas bar gives you a generous plate of tapas with every drink. The portions are surprisingly large for the price, making it perfect for anyone wanting good food without spending much, and its lively atmosphere makes it especially popular with students.

Disfrutar (Romantic)

Disfrutar is perfect for a romantic night out. The food is creative, beautifully presented, and the atmosphere is fancy but still relaxed, making it a great place to enjoy time together without feeling too formal.

El Jardín de la Máquina (Family)

El Jardín de la Máquina is a lovely place for a family meal. The food is delicious with options for everyone, the atmosphere is relaxed and friendly, and it makes enjoying time together easy and stress-free.

Gastroescaperoom (Adventure)

Gastroescaperoom is a fun and unique place where you solve puzzles and challenges while enjoying great food, combining an escape room with a dining experience that’s exciting, different, and perfect for a memorable outing with friends.

Landmarks

Playa d’en Bossa (Student)

Playa d’en Bossa is one of the most popular holiday spots in Ibiza, known for its long beach, bars, clubs, watersports, beach clubs, and its short distance to Ibiza Town and the airport, offering both lively party areas and quieter spots.

Park Güell (Romantic)

Park Güell feels like stepping into a fairy-tale with its colorful mosaics, playful shapes, and unique Gaudí architecture, offering beautiful views over Barcelona and lots of creative details everywhere you look.

City of Arts and Sciences (Family)

The City of Arts and Sciences in Valencia is a huge modern cultural complex with a science museum, planetarium, opera house, and Europe’s largest aquarium, known for its futuristic white buildings and popularity with families.

Caminito del Rey (Adventure)

Caminito del Rey is an almost 8-kilometre path once known as one of the most dangerous in the world, now safely restored but still offering an amazing experience walking along high cliffside pathways with unforgettable views.
