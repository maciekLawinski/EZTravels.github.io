Grafton Hotel (Romantic Getaway)

The Grafton Hotel, located in the heart of Dublin City is a popular destination for anyone

travelling. It features 24-hour desk and room service, accompanied along with a restaurant and

free Wi-Fi throughout the building. A perfect stay for guests who enjoy intimate dining, theatres

and an active nightlife. The perfect getaway for a perfect pair.

Fota-Island Resort (Family Vacation)

Fota-Island Resort introduces an escape for families for a retreat like no other. Guests can

unwind and enjoy all amenities that it has to offer, featuring a WildLife Park for nature

enjoyers. A golf course for those who enjoy a leisurely afternoon and lounges to cool off

after a long day. Families will love it here in Cork, regardless of the season.

Delphi Resort (Adventurous Outing)

At Delphi Resort the skies the limit when it comes to their vast selection of activities. For

the true thrill seekers, the resort offers zip-lining, hiking, biking and even kayaking.

Being able to get out to wherever and whenever is an experience freeing like no other.

Located in Galway, it's the best peak you'll ever climb.

Abbey-Court Hostel (Student-Life)

With an affordable and convenient location, Abbey-Court Hostel is one of Dublin's

go to spots for students who are travelling. Meeting all the necessary requirements,

Even with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities and comfortably sized rooms, Abbey-Court offers

a communal kitchen for anyone who wishes to prepare their own meals.

Cliffs of Moher

The Cliffs Of Moher are a magical place, one where you stand at the edge, overseeing

the Atlantic. No matter when you visit it will retain its appeal, brimming with the

glistening reflection that hits of the ocean currents. Amazing viewpoints await along

the route with many different spots to sit, enjoy and relax oneself.

Fota Wildlife Park

Fota Wildlife Park delivers plenty for families and offers kids many opportunities to

explore. From giraffes, lions and tigers, ostriches and pandas, they care for many various

animals around the park. There are fun trails and play areas that allow children to spend

an unforgettable day there. Free roaming wallabies are about so be quick to catch a glimpse.

The Atlantic Way

Spanning over the western coast, the Atlantic Way stretches a whopping 2,500 km from a rural

town in Inishtowen. Be it hiking mountainous terrain or surfing the rapid waters, the Wild Atlantic

brings a new thrill and a breath of fresh air. For those who crave that rush this is a must-visit,

every turning corner shows off Ireland's scenic beauty to the fullest
